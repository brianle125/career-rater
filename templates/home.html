What is Career Rater?

Career Rater allows employees of a company to safely and anonymously rate companies and workplaces online. Our goal is creating a safer workplace in addition to improving job security. Rating are based off of factors such as job stability, satisfaction, environment, and others. Ratings are then posted online for public viewing.
